Boruto

The story of the Boruto: Naruto Next Generations anime revolves around Naruto, who was a young shinobi with supernatural talent who was able to achieve his dream of becoming the best ninja in the village, so that his face would then be pushed over the statue of the Hokage. In this anime, a new generation of ninja will appear, ready to take the torch and be led by his son Naruto: Boruto!
